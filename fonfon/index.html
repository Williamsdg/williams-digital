Est. 2000

The Spirit of France

Nestled in Birmingham’s beloved Five Points South, Chez Fonfon is a bustling, casual bistro celebrating the timeless traditions of French cuisine. From the Belle Époque café tables to the 100-year-old etched glass door sourced from Lyon, every detail transports you to the heart of Paris.

For over two decades, Chef Frank Stitt and the Fonfon family have served pitch-perfect bistro fare—honest, soulful dishes crafted with care and presented with warmth.

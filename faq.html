Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our ecommerce development services

What platform do you use to build the ecommerce store? ▼

We primarily work with WooCommerce (WordPress), Shopify, and custom-built solutions. During our initial consultation, we'll recommend the best platform based on your business needs and budget.

Do I need to provide the content (images, text, products)? ▼

Yes, please provide your product images, descriptions, and any branding materials. If you need help with content, we offer content upload as an add-on service for $30.

Will my store be mobile-friendly? ▼

Absolutely. Every store we build is fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. Mobile commerce accounts for over 50% of online sales, so this is a top priority for every project.

Which payment gateways do you integrate? ▼

We integrate all major payment gateways including Stripe, PayPal, Square, and Apple Pay. If you need a specific gateway, just let us know and we'll make it happen.

Do you provide support after delivery? ▼

We provide full support during the revision period included in your package. For ongoing maintenance and support beyond that, contact us about a custom maintenance plan.

Can I sell digital products or only physical products? ▼

Both! We can set up your store to sell physical products, digital downloads, subscriptions, or a mix of all three. Just let us know your needs during the consultation.

Will I be able to manage the store myself after delivery? ▼

Yes. We hand over full admin access and provide a brief walkthrough so you can manage products, orders, and settings on your own. No technical skills required.

Do you provide a domain and hosting? ▼

Domain and hosting are not included by default, but the Pro Store package includes hosting setup. We can guide you on the best options or set it up for an additional $50.

How does the milestone payment system work? ▼

Instead of paying everything upfront, your project is divided into milestones. You pay for each milestone as it's completed and approved. This gives you visibility into progress and ensures you only pay for work that meets your expectations.

What if I need more products or pages than my package includes? ▼

No problem! We offer add-on services: additional pages are $20 each, additional products are $5 each, and additional plugins are $10 each. You can also upgrade to a higher package at any time. View all add-ons.

Can I get my store built faster? ▼

Yes! We offer rush delivery for all packages. Starter Store can be delivered in 2 days (+$50), Growth Store in 4 days (+$100), and Pro Store in 6 days (+$150). View fast delivery options.

Still Have Questions?

We're happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
